Relief for Women Strong Enough to Leave

The goal of Lifelines is to empower you to make informed decisions regarding your need for safety, self-esteem and acceptance. Lawyers, counselors and other Lifelines resources are specially trained in domestic abuse issues. In a safe and friendly environment, a counselor can provide you with information about domestic abuse and the support needed to examine your relationship.

Decide to Leave

Making a decision to leave can be complicated. Using Lifelines, you can reach someone who can help you talk through the many factors that can contribute to a decision to leave, as well as practical details that can help ensure your safety.

Find Counsel

Lifelines provides a selection of lawyers, counselors and other resources available through anonymous chat. Talk to someone who understands, safely and anonymously without compromising your identity.

Improve

Through Lifelines, you can reach out and get advice that can help you make better choices over time. Change does not happen overnight, but with the right people in your corner, the future will look brighter.
